03 Blockchain Transparency

The Solana blockchain is a public ledger. Any transaction you submit is permanently recorded and visible. This includes your wallet address, tokens swapped, amounts, and timestamp. Wallet addresses should be treated as pseudonymous, not anonymous.

04 Third-Party Integrations

The Interface may use RPC providers, Jupiter Quote API, and hosting/CDN (e.g. Cloudflare). Each has its own privacy policy. Transaction data and wallet address may be transmitted to RPC providers to submit and read blockchain data.

05 Cookies & Local Storage

We may use localStorage or session storage for preferences (e.g. slippage, selected tokens). This stays in your browser and is not sent to our servers. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

06 How We Use Information

Information is used only to operate and improve the Interface, detect abuse, respond to communications, and comply with law. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information for advertising.

07 Data Retention

Server log data is retained for a limited period (e.g. 30–90 days). On-chain data is permanently retained by the Solana network and outside our control.
